Project Timeline

Milestone 1 – Systems Thinking & Decision Tools

Duration: Week 1 to Week 4

In this phase, I developed resources demonstrating the application of systems thinking using tools like causal loop diagrams and stock-flow models. For example, I analyzed the interdependencies within a healthcare delivery system to understand bottlenecks in patient care pathways (Meadows, 2008). I also evaluated decision-making scenarios using trade-off matrices, enabling structured prioritization of leadership strategies.

Owner: Vinod Kattinapudi

Milestone 2 – Team Leadership & Communication Skills

Duration: Week 5 to Week 8

During this milestone, I designed communication frameworks for team coordination. I incorporated the SBI (Situation-Behavior-Impact) feedback model to improve team discussions and coaching conversations (Center for Creative Leadership, 2021). For example, I facilitated retrospectives using this model, which led to actionable feedback loops and stronger team morale.

Owner: Vinod Kattinapudi

Milestone 3 – Ethical Leadership, Culture, and Final Presentation

Duration: Week 9 to Week 12

This milestone focused on ethical decision-making and organizational culture. I reflected on real-world examples, such as Johnson & Johnson’s response to the Tylenol crisis, to understand integrity in action (Kaptein, 2015). I also integrated Hofstede’s cultural dimensions theory to adapt leadership behaviors across cultural contexts. This work culminated in the final presentation and website deployment.

Owner: Vinod Kattinapudi
